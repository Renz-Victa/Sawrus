Pricing FAQ

Questions about pricing

Is there a free initial consultation?

Yes. We offer a free 30-minute discovery call to understand your situation and confirm we're a good fit before any commitment.

Can we spread the payments for an AI Strategy only?

Yes. Our standard payment structure are 50% upfront and 50% on delivery.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. The engagement covers everything: all discovery sessions, assessment work, use case development, roadmap creation, the executive presentation and debrief meetings. The price you see is the price you pay. No surprise add-ons.

What if we need to pause the engagement?

We offer one free pause of up to 2 weeks. If you need to cancel, we invoice only for work completed up to that point.
