Pricing FAQ

Questions about pricing

Is there a free initial consultation?

Yes. We offer a free 30-minute discovery call to understand your situation and confirm we're a good fit before any commitment.

Can we spread the payments?

Our standard terms are 50% upfront and 50% on delivery. For Enterprise engagements, a 3-payment structure is available upon request.

Are there any hidden fees?

None. The price you see is the price you pay. Travel, tooling, and all materials are included. No surprise add-ons.

What if we need to pause the engagement?

Life happens. We offer one free pause of up to 2 weeks. If you need to cancel, we'll invoice only for work completed up to that point.
