Our Mission

We believe every startup deserves a clear AI strategy... not just the well-funded ones.

The AI consulting market is broken for startups. Large firms charge enterprise rates, move slowly and produce strategy decks full of generic recommendations. Founders end up guessing, wasting time, and missing the window to build a real competitive advantage. Sawrus was built to fix that.

What We Stand For

Four principles that guide every engagement.

1

Honesty

If AI isn't the right investment for your company at this stage, we'll tell you that. We never oversell an engagement to protect our fee. Our reputation is built on clients who trust us to give them the unvarnished truth.

2

Speed

Startups move fast and fundraising windows close. We designed our entire engagement model around a 3–5 week timeline, because we know you can't wait 3 months for a strategy deck.

3

Independence

We have no commercial relationships with AI vendors, cloud providers, or tooling companies. Our build vs buy vs partner recommendations are based entirely on what's right for your specific business.

4

Clarity over complexity

We believe a good strategy should be simple enough for your entire company to understand. If your roadmap needs a PhD to read, it won't get executed. Every deliverable is written for the whole team, not just the CTO.
