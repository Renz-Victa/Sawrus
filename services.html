Engagement Detail

What the 5 weeks look like

Week 1

Kick-off & Discovery

Structured interviews with your leadership, product, engineering, and data teams. We map your current tech stack, data assets, and business objectives — no fluff, just facts.

Week 2

AI Maturity Assessment

We score your readiness across five dimensions: data quality, infrastructure, team capability, governance maturity, and competitive positioning. You get a raw, honest score.

Week 3

Use Case Identification

We identify 8–15 viable AI use cases for your business, scoring each by estimated ROI, implementation complexity, strategic fit, and time to value.

Week 4

Strategy & Roadmap Design

We develop your prioritised 12–18 month AI roadmap, including build vs buy analysis, team capability requirements, and risk mitigation framework.

Week 5

Delivery, Presentation & Handover

We deliver a complete board-ready executive presentation, a detailed technical roadmap document, and run a full readout with your leadership team. You also receive 30-day post-delivery email support for questions.

FAQ

Common questions

Do we need to have existing AI infrastructure?

Not at all. Many clients come to us with zero AI in production. Our assessment works at any stage — the output simply reflects where you are and the fastest path forward.

How much access do you need to our systems?

We work through structured interviews and documentation reviews. We do not need direct database or system access — protecting your IP and security is a priority.

Can this be used for our next funding round?

Yes. Many founders use the output to demonstrate a credible AI strategy to Series A/B investors. We can tailor the executive presentation to your investor audience.

What happens after the engagement ends?

You receive all deliverables and 30 days of post-delivery email support. Many clients also return for a follow-up check-in engagement 6–12 months later.
