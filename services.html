Engagement Detail

What the 5 weeks look like

WEEK 1

Kick-off & Discovery

Structured interviews with your leadership, product, engineering, and data teams. We map your current tech stack, data assets and business objectives.

WEEK 2

AI Maturity Assessment

We score your readiness across five dimensions: data quality, infrastructure, team capability, governance maturity and competitive positioning.

WEEK 3

Use Case Identification

We identify 8–15 viable AI use cases, scoring each by estimated ROI, implementation complexity, strategic fit and time to value.

WEEK 4

Strategy & Roadmap Design

We develop your prioritised 12–18 month AI roadmap including build vs buy analysis, team requirements and risk mitigation framework.

WEEK 5

Delivery, Presentation & Handover

Complete board-ready executive presentation, detailed technical roadmap and a full readout session with your leadership. You also receive 30-day post-delivery email support.

Common Questions

Frequently asked

Do we need existing AI infrastructure?

Not at all. Many clients come with zero AI in production. Our assessment works at any stage — the output reflects where you are and the fastest path forward.

How much system access do you need?

We work through structured interviews and documentation reviews. We don't need direct database or system access — protecting your IP and security is a priority.

Can this be used for our funding round?

Yes. Many founders use the output to demonstrate a credible AI strategy to Series A/B investors. We can tailor the executive presentation to your investor audience.

What happens after the engagement ends?

You receive all deliverables and 30 days of post-delivery email support. Many clients return for a follow-up check-in engagement 6–12 months later.
